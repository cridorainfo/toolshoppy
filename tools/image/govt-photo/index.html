Frequently asked questions

What is the correct photo size for Kerala PSC applications?

Kerala PSC typically requires a 3.5cm x 4.5cm photo (around 276x354 px) under 50KB on a plain white background. Select the Kerala PSC preset, upload your photo, and download — it's auto-cropped to the exact size.

Can I use this for UPSC, SSC, PAN, and Aadhaar photos too?

Yes. Presets are included for UPSC, SSC, PAN Card, Aadhaar, Indian Passport, UAE Emirates ID, UK Passport, and US Passport — each with the correct pixel dimensions and file size limit.

Why does the tool add a background color?

Many government forms require a plain white, light blue, or light red background. Choose your required background before uploading, and the tool centers and pads your photo onto it.

Are these presets officially guaranteed to be accepted?

Presets follow commonly published requirements, but portals occasionally change specs. Always verify the exact size and file limit on your specific application form before final submission.
