PSC photo specifications

Kerala Public Service Commission (PSC) online applications require a passport-style photograph with strict dimensions. The standard size is 3.5 cm × 4.5 cm (approximately 276 × 354 pixels at 200 DPI) with a maximum file size of 50 KB in JPEG format on a plain white background.

Common rejection reasons include wrong dimensions, file size over 50KB, coloured or patterned backgrounds, and blurry or side-profile photos. The photo must show a clear, front-facing view with both ears visible and no sunglasses or headwear (except religious).
