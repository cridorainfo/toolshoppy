UPSC photo specifications

Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) online applications for Civil Services, CDS, NDA, and other exams require a passport-style photograph at 200 × 230 pixels with a maximum file size of 300 KB in JPEG format.

The photo must be recent (taken within the last 6 months), show a clear front-facing view, and have a plain white or light-coloured background. Half-body or side-profile photos are rejected. Both ears should be visible and no headwear except for religious reasons.

Frequently asked questions

What is the UPSC photo size?

UPSC online applications typically require a 200×230 pixel photograph with a maximum file size of 300KB in JPEG format on a plain white or light-coloured background.

What background colour does UPSC require?

UPSC requires a plain white or light-coloured background. Avoid patterned, dark, or coloured backgrounds that may cause portal rejection.
