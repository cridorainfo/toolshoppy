Indian passport photo specifications

Indian passport applications through the Passport Seva portal require a square photograph measuring 2 × 2 inches (51 × 51 mm), which translates to approximately 350 × 350 pixels at standard DPI, with a maximum file size of 200 KB.

The photo must have a plain white background, show a full front-facing view with both eyes open, and be taken within the last 6 months. No sunglasses, hats, or uniforms (except religious headwear). The face should occupy 70–80% of the frame.
