Frequently asked questions

How do I convert HEIC to JPG for free?

Upload your HEIC photo, select JPG as the output format, and click Convert All. Conversion happens in your browser — no upload, no signup.

Can I convert multiple images at once?

Yes. Drag and drop multiple files at once, pick your target format, and download them all together with one click.

Why did my HEIC file fail to convert?

HEIC decoding depends on your browser and operating system. It works out of the box on Safari and recent Chrome on Mac/Windows 11. If it fails, try exporting the photo as JPG from your Photos app first.

What formats are supported?

You can convert to JPG, PNG, or WebP. Supported source formats include JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, BMP, and HEIC (browser-dependent).
