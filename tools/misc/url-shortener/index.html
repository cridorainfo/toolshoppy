Frequently asked questions

Is this URL shortener free?

Yes. Shorten unlimited links for free — no signup, no account, and no hidden limits.

Do the short links expire?

No. Short links are permanent and will keep working indefinitely once created.

Can I choose my own short link name?

Yes — enter a custom alias (5-30 letters, numbers or underscores). If it's already taken, try a different one.

Does ToolShoppy store or track my links?

No. ToolShoppy has no backend for this tool — your link is sent directly from your browser to a trusted shortening service to create the redirect. Your history is saved only in your own browser's local storage.
