Frequently asked questions

How do I convert JPG to PDF for free?

Upload your JPG images, reorder if needed, pick a page size, and click Create PDF. Download instantly — no server upload.

Can I combine multiple images into one PDF?

Yes. Add as many images as you need. Each image becomes one page in the final PDF, in the order shown.

What image formats are supported?

JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, and BMP. Other formats are converted via canvas before embedding.
