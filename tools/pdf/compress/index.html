How to compress a PDF online

  1. Upload your PDF.
  2. Choose High, Medium, or Low quality.
  3. Click Compress PDF and download the smaller file.

Frequently asked questions

How do I compress a PDF for free?

Upload your PDF, pick a quality preset, click Compress, then download.

Will compression reduce quality?

Lower presets shrink more but look softer. Medium is a good default.

Is my PDF uploaded to a server?

No. Compression runs entirely in your browser.
