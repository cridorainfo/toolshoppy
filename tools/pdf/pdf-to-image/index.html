Frequently asked questions

How do I convert PDF to JPG for free?

Upload your PDF, choose JPG or PNG, pick a quality preset, and click Convert. Download a single image or a ZIP for multi-page PDFs.

What image quality can I expect?

Pages are rendered with PDF.js at up to 2× scale. High quality is best for printing; Medium works well for sharing on WhatsApp.

Is my PDF uploaded to a server?

No. Conversion runs entirely in your browser — your document never leaves your device.
