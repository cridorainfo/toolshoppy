Frequently asked questions

How do I split a PDF into separate pages?

Upload your PDF, choose Every page, and download a ZIP with one PDF per page. Everything runs in your browser.

Can I extract only specific pages?

Yes. Tap page thumbnails to select them, then use Extract selected to save just those pages as one PDF.

Is my PDF uploaded to a server?

No. Splitting uses pdf-lib and PDF.js entirely inside your browser — your file never leaves your device.
