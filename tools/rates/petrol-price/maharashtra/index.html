Petrol price in Maharashtra

Maharashtra is India's largest fuel-consuming state, with Mumbai alone accounting for a significant share of national petrol sales. Fuel prices in Mumbai, Pune, Nagpur, Nashik, and other Maharashtra cities are revised daily at 6 AM by oil marketing companies.

Maharashtra's state VAT on petrol is among the higher rates in India, which contributes to Mumbai petrol prices being above the national average. Fleet operators, cab drivers, and daily commuters across the Mumbai Metropolitan Region track these rates closely.

Frequently asked questions

What is the petrol price in Maharashtra today?

Maharashtra petrol prices are revised daily. This page shows today's petrol and diesel rates per litre for Maharashtra including Mumbai, updated from public fuel price feeds.

Is Mumbai petrol price the same as Pune?

Yes. Petrol and diesel prices are uniform across Maharashtra cities including Mumbai, Pune, Nagpur, and Nashik, as state VAT is applied at the same rate statewide.
