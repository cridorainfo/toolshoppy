Petrol price in Kerala

Kerala fuel prices are revised daily by Indian Oil, BPCL, and HPCL based on international crude oil rates and the rupee-dollar exchange rate. Petrol and diesel prices are uniform across all Kerala districts including Thiruvananthapuram, Ernakulam (Kochi), Kozhikode, Thrissur, and Kollam.

Kerala's state VAT on petrol is among the factors that influence the final pump price. Commuters and fleet operators across the state track daily rate changes to plan fuel expenses. Use our full petrol price tool to compare Kerala with other Indian states.

Diesel prices affect Kerala's transport and fishing industries significantly. Bookmark this page for daily Kerala fuel rate updates.

Frequently asked questions

What is the petrol price in Kerala today?

Kerala petrol prices are set daily by oil marketing companies. This page shows today's petrol and diesel rates per litre for Kerala, updated from public fuel price feeds.

Is petrol price the same across Kerala cities?

Petrol and diesel prices are uniform across Kerala cities including Kochi, Trivandrum, Kozhikode, and Thrissur, as state VAT is applied uniformly.
