Frequently asked questions

What is the gold rate in Kerala today?

Kerala gold rates track the all-India MCX benchmark closely. This page shows today's 22K and 24K rates per gram, updated daily.

How is 22K gold price calculated from 24K?

22K gold is 22/24 parts pure gold. The 22K rate is typically 24K × (22÷24), plus making charges at jewellers.

Is Dubai gold rate shown in AED?

Yes. UAE rates are shown in AED per gram for 24K, 22K and 18K, plus per tola (11.664g) for Dubai market reference.
