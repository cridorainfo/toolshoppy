Gold rate in Mumbai & India

Mumbai is India's largest gold market, centred around Zaveri Bazar — one of Asia's biggest bullion trading districts. Jewellers across Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, and Thane price 22K gold based on the national MCX (Multi Commodity Exchange) benchmark shown on this page.

22K gold is the standard for Indian jewellery — bangles, chains, and rings. The 8-gram sovereign (22K) is a popular investment coin sold by banks and jewellers nationwide. 24K rates are used for gold bars and coins.

India gold rates apply uniformly across major cities including Mumbai, Delhi, Chennai, and Bangalore, with minor local variations. Use our full gold rate tool to compare India, Kerala, and UAE rates.

Frequently asked questions

What is the gold rate in Mumbai today?

Mumbai jewellers follow the national MCX benchmark. This page shows today's India-wide 22K and 24K rates per gram, which Mumbai markets reference.

How is 22K gold price calculated from 24K?

22K gold is 22/24 parts pure gold. The 22K rate is typically 24K × (22÷24), plus making charges at jewellers.
