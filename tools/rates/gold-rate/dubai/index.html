Gold rate in Dubai & UAE

Dubai is one of the world's largest gold trading hubs. The Dubai Gold Souk in Deira sets the benchmark for retail gold prices across the UAE. Rates are quoted in AED (UAE Dirham) per gram for 24K, 22K, and 18K purity, with tola (11.664g) pricing common in traditional shops.

NRIs and Kerala Gulf workers frequently compare Dubai gold rates with India prices before buying. Investment gold in UAE carries zero VAT, making it attractive for remittance-linked purchases. Use our AED to INR converter to compare Dubai rates with Kerala or Mumbai prices.

24K gold bars and coins are the most popular investment format in Dubai. 22K is standard for jewellery. Check the full gold rate tool to switch between UAE, Kerala, and India rates side by side.

Frequently asked questions

Is Dubai gold rate shown in AED?

Yes. UAE rates are shown in AED per gram for 24K, 22K and 18K, plus per tola (11.664g) for Dubai market reference.

Why is Dubai gold popular with NRIs?

Dubai Gold Souk offers competitive AED rates and zero VAT on investment gold. Many NRIs buy gold in UAE and compare with Kerala/India rates using AED to INR conversion.
