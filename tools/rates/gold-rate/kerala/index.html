Gold rate in Kerala

Kerala has one of India's highest per-capita gold consumption rates. Malayali households track daily gold prices closely — especially during wedding season (Makaram–Meenam months), Onam, and Vishu when gold purchases peak across Thrissur, Kochi, Kozhikode, and Trivandrum.

Kerala jewellers price 22K gold jewellery based on the national MCX (Multi Commodity Exchange) benchmark. The 22K rate shown above is what most Kerala families reference before visiting local jewellers. Making charges, GST, and design premiums are added on top of the base rate.

22K gold is the standard for Kerala wedding jewellery — thali chains, bangles, and kasu mala. 24K is preferred for gold coins and sovereign (8g) investment purchases. Use our full gold rate tool to switch between Kerala, India, and UAE rates.

Frequently asked questions

What is the gold rate in Kerala today?

Kerala gold rates track the all-India MCX benchmark closely. This page shows today's 22K and 24K rates per gram, updated daily.

Why do Kerala families check gold rates daily?

Gold is central to Kerala weddings, festivals like Vishu, and family investments. Daily rate checks help plan purchases and compare jeweller prices before buying.
